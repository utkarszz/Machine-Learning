 What is K-Means?

> "K-Means dataset ko groups (clusters) me divide karta hai, based on similarity."



K = number of groups (clusters)
Means = mean of values (center point / centroid)

Example:

Mall me customers ke data (age, spending score) se model khud pata lagata hai ki:

Group A: students

Group B: middle age high spenders

Group C: low spend customers




---

 Steps of K-Means (very simple)

1. Decide number of clusters (K)


2. Random K points select → inhe bolte hain centroids


3. Each data point ko nearest centroid se assign karo


4. Centroid update karo (mean ke hisaab se)


5. Until centroids stop moving → repeat
